About us

Brief History

Vision

A generation of disciplined citizens whose character influences society.

Our Mission

Training Students to set and achieve own goals from early age through a Christian-based individualised Curriculum supported by Supervisors and Parents who model Christ-like character

Our Core Values:

Responsibility - I take charge of my environment to realise my God given destiny.

Integrity - I am honest and truthful.

Discipline - I practice self control and obey the rules.

Excellence - I do my best to realise my full potential.

Service - I look for way to help others.

High school Grade(8 - 12)

Students undertake their A.C.E learning towards achieving the International Certificate of Christian Education (ICCE). The ICCE is a baccalaureate awarding system that qualifies each individual student based on their overall perfomance. there are five ICCE basic certificate
